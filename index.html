Stephanie Reeves

I work with Dr. Jorge Otero-Millan in the Ocular Motor Lab at UC Berkeley. My research focuses on how visual perception and the ocular motor system are influenced by changes in the alignment between the eyes, head, and direction of gravity. I use custom-built eye trackers, HMDs, and stereoscopic (haploscope) displays to improve our understanding of eye movements and vision. Welcome!
